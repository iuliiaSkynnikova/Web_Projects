Camping

The openness and flat sites at sunny spot holidays make this park a great choice for your holiday. Some of the sites are positioned in elevated positions with views over the valley. There are some sites close to the beach.

Sunny spot holidays features:

  • 18 cabins
  • 50 powered grass sites
  • 90 powered slab sites
  • Boat parking
